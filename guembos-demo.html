Our Story

Born in the Heart of Allapattah

Guembo's and Grill started as what all great things start as: a passion for real food and a desire to feed the community right. Located on NW 22nd Ave, we've been a fixture in Allapattah — one of Miami's most authentic, hard-working neighborhoods.

Everything we make comes from recipes passed down and perfected over time. We believe good food should be bold, honest, and made with pride. No shortcuts. Just smoke, heat, and flavor that keeps people coming back.

Whether you're a longtime regular or pulling up for the first time, you'll feel it from the first bite. That's the Guembo's promise.

— The Guembo's Family
